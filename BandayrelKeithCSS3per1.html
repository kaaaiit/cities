London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

London's ancient core, the City of London, largely retains its 1.12 square-mile medieval boundaries. The bulk of this conurbation forms Greater London, a region of England governed by the Mayor of London and London Assembly.
